By Tarantino

It was 1969, and Hollywood was changing, but they were relics of a bygone era...

This film is a sprawling, sun-soaked journey through a city on the brink of change. Tarantino blends nostalgia with tension, capturing the essence of a bygone era while building toward a shocking and unexpected climax. Leonardo DiCaprio and Brad Pitt are at the top of their game, delivering performances that are both heartfelt and hilarious.

Expect everything you love about Tarantino: sharp dialogue, memorable characters, and an atmosphere that oozes with 60s style. The film is packed with homages to the cinema and television of the time, and it’s all tied together with a killer soundtrack that brings the era to life. *Once Upon a Time in Hollywood* is a tale of friendship, change, and the end of an era, all wrapped up in Tarantino’s unique vision.
