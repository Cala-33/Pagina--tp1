By Tarantino

He was a freed slave with one goal—to find and save his wife...

Django Unchained is Tarantino’s western with a vengeance. Set in the antebellum South, it follows Django, a former slave turned bounty hunter, on his quest to rescue his wife from a brutal plantation owner. Alongside him is Dr. King Schultz, a German bounty hunter with a silver tongue and a deadly aim.

This film is a mix of bloody action, sharp social commentary, and that unmistakable Tarantino dialogue. Jamie Foxx and Christoph Waltz shine as the dynamic duo, while Leonardo DiCaprio’s Calvin Candie and Samuel L. Jackson’s Stephen offer up some truly sinister performances. The film’s a blend of spaghetti western influences, intense shootouts, and a powerful story of love and revenge.

Django is Tarantino at his most epic, delivering a story that’s as thrilling as it is thought-provoking, all while tipping its hat to the classic westerns of old.

Running Time: 2h 45m

Released: 2012

Genre: Western/Action
