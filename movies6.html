By Tarantino

They were just out for a good time, but he turned their night into a nightmare...

Death Proof is Tarantino’s twisted take on the slasher genre, where the killer isn’t wielding a knife but driving a “death proof” muscle car. The film follows Stuntman Mike, a psychotic stunt driver who preys on unsuspecting women, turning their joyrides into deadly encounters.

This film is a throwback to grindhouse cinema, packed with explosive car chases, intense dialogue, and Tarantino’s signature brand of dark humor. The first half of the movie builds tension through a mix of creepy charm and casual conversation, while the second half is a high-octane chase that flips the script in the best way possible.

Expect brutal action, killer stunts, and a soundtrack that’ll have you tapping your foot as you grip the edge of your seat. *Death Proof* is a wild ride that’s as much about revenge as it is about survival, delivered with Tarantino’s unmistakable style.

Running Time: 1h 50m

Released: 2007
